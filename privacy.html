Privacy Policy

Colorful Snake Game — by Kims Games

Effective date: 2025-08-22

This policy describes how the iOS app Colorful Snake Game (“the App”) handles your data. We do not collect personally identifiable information from the App. The App is designed to serve non‑personalized ads only via Google AdMob and does not use the IDFA or any tracking that links data across apps or websites.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account creation or login.
  • No name, email, phone number, or precise location.
  • No device advertising identifier (IDFA) access.
  • No analytics SDKs or data brokers.

Advertising (Non‑Personalized)

The App shows banner/interstitial ads using Google AdMob in non‑personalized mode. This means Google does not use your data to build a profile or show targeted ads. AdMob may process limited technical data necessary to deliver and measure ads (e.g., approximate location, device type, IP for geolocation & fraud prevention). We do not enable tracking and we request consent where required through Google’s UMP (User Messaging Platform).

You can change your ad consent at any time using the in‑app Privacy Options button (Highscores screen).

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

The App does not request ATT permission because we do not collect data used to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies.

Children

The App is a casual game and not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Rights

If you have any questions, wish to request deletion of any data that could be associated with you, or want clarification about AdMob’s data processing, contact us using the email below.
