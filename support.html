FAQ

Ads aren’t showing
Ads may take some time to begin after initial release or can be limited by network connectivity or ad availability. We only show non‑personalized ads.

How do I reset highscores?
Go to Highscores and tap Clear Highscores.

How do I change ad consent?
Open the Highscores screen and tap Privacy Options to manage consent via Google’s UMP.
