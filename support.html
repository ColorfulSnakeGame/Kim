FAQ

No sound? Ensure Silent Mode is off and the in‑game audio toggle is enabled. The app respects iOS Silent Mode.

Haptics? Haptics can be toggled in-game. Availability depends on your iPhone model.

Progress & scores? High scores are stored locally on your device only.

Device support? iOS 14.0 or later (optimized for iPhone).
